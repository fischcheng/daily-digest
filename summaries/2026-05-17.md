## Sustainability & Climate

- **[New dbt Labs Report Finds AI-driven Acceleration is Outpacing Trust and Governance](https://www.getdbt.com/blog/new-dbt-labs-report-finds-ai-driven-acceleration-is-outpacing-trust-and-governance)**
  - Source: dbt Labs
  - The 2026 State of Analytics Engineering Report finds that 72% of data teams use AI-assisted coding while 71% fear hallucinated outputs reaching stakeholders, revealing a structural governance gap where pipeline output speed is outpacing the validation and data-quality mechanisms organisations depend on.

- **[Agentic Data Pipelines: AI-Driven Autonomous Data Engineering in 2026](https://www.ishir.com/blog/320917/agentic-data-pipelines-the-shift-to-autonomous-data-engineering.htm)**
